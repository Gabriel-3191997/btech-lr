BUSHROD COLLEGE OF SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY

Our academic programs span across diverse fields including engineering, technology, healthcare, and management. We foster an environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and hands-on learning, preparing our graduates to excel in their chosen professions. Students at Bushrod College benefit from state-of-the-art facilities, cutting edge laboratories, and access to industry-leading research opportunities. Our campus provides a supportive community where students are inspired to achieve their full potential. Our commitment to quality education extends beyond the classroom. We prioritize student success and offer comprehensive support services including career counseling, internships, and networking opportunities.


Apply Online

BTECH recommends that students submit their application to our admissions office online. It takes 10 minutes to fill out a BTECH application. Our admissions officers will respond to your application typically within 30 days.
